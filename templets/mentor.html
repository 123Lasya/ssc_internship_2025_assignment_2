Become an SSC Mentor

Guide students. Share your journey. Grow as a leader. Join SSC's mission today.

Why Mentor?
  • Make an impact in student careers
  • Get SSC certification and LinkedIn recognition
  • Build your mentoring and leadership profile
Who Can Apply?
  • Professionals from any industry
  • Educators, researchers, community builders
  • Final-year students with proven experience
  • SSC Alumni & Contributors
What Will You Do?
  • Host online sessions or Q&As
  • Mentor students on projects, careers, or skills
  • Support SSC events and activities
